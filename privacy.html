Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2024

Information We Collect

Stoichiometry Calculator is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information.
  • Local Storage Only: Your preferences and equation history are stored locally in your browser and never transmitted to our servers.
  • No Account Required: The calculator works entirely in your browser without requiring registration or login.

How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal information:

  • We cannot and do not track your usage patterns
  • We cannot and do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We cannot and do not use your data for advertising purposes

Local Browser Storage

The application may store the following data locally in your browser:

  • Calculation mode preferences (balance, redox, mass)
  • UI preferences and settings
  • Recent equation history (for your convenience)

This data remains on your device and can be cleared by clearing your browser's local storage.

Third-Party Services

This website is hosted on Netlify. Please refer to Netlify's Privacy Policy for information about their data practices.

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or other tracking services
  • Social media pixels
  • Advertising networks
  • Third-party cookies

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies. The application functions entirely through client-side JavaScript without external data transmission.

Data Security

Since all calculations are performed locally in your browser:

  • Your chemical equations never leave your device
  • No network transmission of calculation data occurs
  • The application works offline after initial load

Children's Privacy

This service is safe for users of all ages as we do not collect any personal information. The calculator is designed to be educational and appropriate for students.

Open Source

This calculator is open source, meaning the code is publicly available for inspection. You can verify our privacy practices by examining the source code.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Since we don't collect contact information, we recommend bookmarking this page to check for updates.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can:

  • Review the open source code to verify our practices
  • Check browser developer tools to confirm no external data transmission
  • Use browser privacy tools to monitor network activity

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, typical data rights (access, deletion, portability) don't apply. However, you have full control over any local data by managing your browser settings.

Summary: This calculator prioritizes your privacy by operating entirely in your browser without collecting, storing, or transmitting any personal information.
